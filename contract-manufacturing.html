CONTRACT
MANUFACTURING

BCI’s expertise and extensive infrastructure have attracted business partners who wish to reduce costs, increase, or maximize in-country value, or focus on their commercial operations.

The Contract Manufacturing division provides access to BCI’s extensive expertise in chemical manufacturing and supply chain infrastructure with a complete end-to-end template of services or bespoke solutions catered to requirements and business needs.

This includes but is not limited to:

Raw material procurement, including importation, customs clearance, warehousing and management of inventories.
Production of finished product to business partners’ exact specifications and methods with broad experience in various temperature-controlled mixing for aqueous, solvent-based, and powder-based batch processing.
Warehousing of finished products including climate controlled warehouses.
User-ready packaging and labelling.
Delivery and export services to partners’ facilities or end user sites.
Protection mechanisms of principal Intellectual Property related to product formulations, markets, and other related commercial aspects.
