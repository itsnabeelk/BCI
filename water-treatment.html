WATER
TREATMENT

BCI’s Water Treatment division provides total solutions to prevent operational problems, protect customer assets, and maximize efficiencies.

We provide on-site water testing to troubleshoot and diagnose system problems, as well as access to our analytical laboratory, used for more specialized water testing and performance testing of new applications.

BCI chemistries include:

Oxygen scavengers
Corrosion and scale inhibitors
Dispersants, biocides, and algaecide
Antifoams
Flocculants and coagulants
Activated carbons
Ion-exchange resins
Ferric chloride 40%

The Water Treatment Chemicals division serves the water and wastewater sectors with:

Desalination Plants - Reverse Osmosis and MSF
Waste and effluent treatment systems
Boiler water systems
Cooling and chilling systems
Potable and firewater systems
